# Contributing to Anvil

Thanks for your interest in contributing to Anvil! Here are the rules:

1. Think about whether this should be an issue or a PR. I am *not* more likely to merge a small PR faster than I would implement a small issue.
2. AI is fine, but whether or not you use it, write an AI disclosure in your pull request. I expect something like: "I did not use AI"; "I one-shot this with AI"; "I researched the codebase using AI but wrote the code by hand"; or something similar. Provide as much detail as you can.
3. Often I will receive a PR and realise it is faster for me to rewrite it than to discuss improvements with you. So include either of the following statements in your PR:
   1. I just want this problem solved - If you write this, I will decide how to handle the PR and action it myself.
   2. I want to own solving this problem in a high-effort way - If you write this, I assume you either want to learn about code style and approaches from me for your personal growth (I don't claim to be a great person to learn from!) and/or to become a regular Anvil contributor. I will put in the effort to give you feedback and ownership of the PR. If you're going to just put my feedback into an LLM and update the PR based on what the LLM says, for God's sake don't pick this option.
4. Please say whether you want to maintain this feature after it is merged. This doesn't at all influence whether the PR will be merged; it tells me whether I should bother you with issues in the future or not!
5. I reserve the right to manage branches in such a way that your authorship is not included in the git history. I will do my *best* not to do this, and I will *always* give credit in the changelog; this rule is to protect myself from situations like: you make a PR, tell me you want to put in effort so I don't merge it, then don't keep up with development, leaving me in merge-conflict hell if I decide I want to fix the issue myself. If you're not comfortable with this, don't make a PR!

If you do not follow these rules (especially the *3* items required in your PR description), I won't look at your PR; I'll link you to this document. Add the appropriate info, then ping me and I'll take a look and get back to you.
